Frequently Asked Questions

Our ptarmigans are digitally enhanced with carefully designed cardigans using advanced imaging techniques. No actual ptarmigans were involved in cardigan-wearing during the creation of this collection, ensuring their comfort and natural behavior remains undisturbed.
The concept emerged from the delightful contrast between Arctic wildlife and cozy knitwear. We wanted to celebrate both the natural beauty of ptarmigans and the timeless appeal of well-crafted cardigans, creating a whimsical intersection of nature and fashion.
While our featured cardigans are conceptual designs created specifically for our ptarmigan models, we're exploring partnerships with artisan knitwear designers to bring human-sized versions to life. Stay tuned for updates!
Each ptarmigan is selected based on their distinctive personality and natural poise. We look for birds that embody the spirit of sophistication and charm that pairs beautifully with our cardigan designs. It's about finding the perfect match between bird and knitwear aesthetic.
Ptarmigans are remarkably adaptable birds that naturally change their plumage with the seasons, demonstrating an innate understanding of appropriate seasonal styling. This natural fashion sense makes them perfect candidates for our cardigan collection.
We're always on the lookout for new ptarmigan models and cardigan designs. Our collection grows organically as we discover birds with exceptional style potential and develop new knitwear concepts that complement their natural beauty.
Ptarmigans are exceptionally well-adapted to cold climates with their natural insulation. Our digital cardigans are purely aesthetic additions that celebrate their inherent coziness rather than providing additional warmth they don't need.
